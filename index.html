About Me

Hi, I'm Fang Cheng, a Ph.D. student in Data Science at the City University of Hong Kong. I received my master's degree in Computer Science and bachelor's degree in Data Science and Big Data Technology from Northwestern Polytechnical University.

My research focuses on resource-efficient mobile and edge AI systems. I work on runtime-efficient sparse updates, adaptive sparsity, and heterogeneous mobile processor inference, with recent projects in responsive test-time adaptation for non-stationary mobile environments, edge-cloud collaborative domain mining, weather-oriented semantic segmentation, and memory-efficient fine-tuning for Multi-LoRA LLMs.

  • Current: Ph.D. in Data Science, City University of Hong Kong.
  • Recent work: CVPR 2026 TaskIT and IEEE TMC 2026 responsive test-time adaptation.
  • Research interests: mobile AI systems, test-time adaptation, domain adaptation and generalization, and efficient LLM fine-tuning.
